# Arduino_BT_car
This is a repository for arduino_bt controlled car 
in this repository you'll find full code for programming your arduino to have a bluetooth controlled car 
You can adjust speed and directions from "arduino bluetooth control" mobile app 
this car has a bt module,an ultrasonic sensor,lcd displayer,motor driver,led and buzzer for alarming puposes
--this car detctes obstacles measures their distance and reduces speed based on the the remaining distance within 10 cm our car stops 
thus you cannot move forward unless the obstacle moves or you may just change direction also the distance is always displayed on the lcd and led and buzzer are on when obstacle is within the threshold distance
